    Shipment processing time



    All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. In case shipment is delayed by a few days due to high volume of orders we will contact you via email.


    Shipping rates & delivery estimates



    Free shipping within Poland.
    For International shipping, please contact for a quote via tiana.breeze.art@gmail.com. Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

    In the event that a package is returned to us due to error on the customer's end (i.e. mistaken shipping address), the fees to re-ship the original package will be invoiced to the customer. If you made a mistake with your order, please fill out the Contact Form as soon as possible.

    Customs, Duties and Taxes



    If you are ordering a painting outside of Poland, please be aware of a possibility that you may need to pay custom fees additionally. This varies from country to country, so make sure to familiarize yourself with your country's custom fees. It is possible that you will not be charged at all. This is entirely out of my control and you are responsible for paying any custom fees for your package. Refunds on custom fees are not possible. We are not responsible for delays due to customs.


    Damages



    I use all professional packing materials to make sure your artwork gets to you safely wherever in the world you may live.
    But if you received your order damaged, please contact Tiana Breeze immediately at tiana.breeze.art@gmail.com and provide pictures of damaged packaging to file a claim with the shipment carrier. See our refund and returns policy for any additional info.

    Refund and Returns Policy


    Refunds and returns are NOT ACCEPTED, it is a final sale.

    Refunds are not possible unless the painting has been damaged or lost in transport.

    If you received your order damaged, please contact us immediately at tianabreezeart@gmail.com and provide pictures of damaged packaging and artwork to be eligible for a refund.

    Please take pictures/videos of the packaging BEFORE opening the package and then take photos of the damaged artwork inside in the unlikely event that your delivery was damaged in transit.

    Please provide evidence of the damaged packaging signed by the courier (including surname and signature) - we will have to contact them for confirmation and refund. If you don't contact us same day, we can’t offer you any refund or exchange.

    To be eligible for a return of DAMAGED painting, your item must be in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

    When shipping a painting back, a trackable shipping service MUST be used.

    Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval of your refund.
