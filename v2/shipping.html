    1. Deadlines



    Delivery times will depend on the delivery location. Generally, orders can take from 3 to 10 working days to arrive. Worldwide deliveries take 10-14 working days.


    2. Prices


    Free shipping within Poland. For International shipping, please contact for a quote via tiana.breeze.art@gmail.com. Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.




    Return and Exchange Policy:



    I, Tiana Breeze, take great pride in crafting and delivering exceptional original artwork. Your satisfaction with your purchase is my top priority. Please review the following return and exchange policy:


    1. Satisfaction Guarantee



    I am committed to providing you with art that you love. If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact me within 14 days of receiving your artwork to discuss your concerns.
    In the event that a package is returned to us due to error on the customer's end (i.e. mistaken shipping address), the fees to re-ship the original package will be invoiced to the customer. If you made a mistake with your order, please fill out the Contact Form as soon as possible.


    2. Returns



    In the event that you wish to return your artwork, you may do so within 14 days of receiving it. The artwork must be returned in its original condition and packaging. The cost of return shipping will be the responsibility of the buyer, and I recommend using a reliable shipping method with tracking for returns.


    3. Refunds


    Upon receiving the returned artwork and verifying its condition, I will initiate a refund for the purchase price, minus any shipping and handling charges. Refunds will be processed within 7 days of receiving the returned artwork and will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.


    4. Exchanges


    If you wish to exchange your artwork for another piece from my collection, please contact me to discuss the available options.


    5. Damaged Artwork


    In the rare event that your artwork arrives damaged, please contact me immediately. To assist with any insurance claims, please document the damage by providing clear photographs of both the artwork and the packaging. I will work with you to resolve the issue, whether through a refund, replacement, or repair, depending on the nature of the damage.


    6. Cancellations


    If you need to cancel your order, please do so within 12 hours of placing your order. Cancellations made after this time frame may not be possible if the artwork has already been shipped.


    7.Customs, Duties and Taxes


    If you are ordering a painting outside of Poland, please be aware of a possibility that you may need to pay custom fees additionally. This varies from country to country, so make sure to familiarize yourself with your country's custom fees. It is possible that you will not be charged at all. This is entirely out of my control and you are responsible for paying any custom fees for your package. Refunds on custom fees are not possible. We are not responsible for delays due to customs.


    8. Contact Information


    To initiate a return, exchange, or discuss any concerns, please contact me Contact Form. Your trust in my art is greatly appreciated, and I am committed to providing you with a positive and satisfying experience. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to reach out.
