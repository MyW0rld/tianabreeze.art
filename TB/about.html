    ABOUT MY ART


    Art is my life and passion. They say that to find your style as an artist, you need to find what you love. I am a sea romantic and I love everything that is connected with the sea. I am fascinated by how different it can be. Sea can convey the whole range of feelings: from complete calm and peace to a storm and raging waves. Water can heal and destroy. I like to balance between these two states and paint pictures in accordance with my emotional experiences.

    I like to watch how the lighting on the sea changes, how the color of the boat is reflected in the water, how the glare of the sun flickers on the waves. The play of light and color. And also the spirit of freedom, wind, freshness and sea breeze! Breathtaking, isn't it?..


    Traveling on a yacht is always an endless feeling of freedom and unity with nature. When you look at the sea wave, everything becomes unimportant. It is pure meditation. As if in the whole world there is only you and this majestic beauty. I convey all my love for the sea, yachts, ships, marine life through my paintings. My paintings are like a sea breeze - fresh, light and dreamy.

    "Look at my pictures and just breath..."

    Tiana Breeze
